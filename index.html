Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation services for all your needs

Osteoarthritis

Comprehensive treatment for joint degeneration and pain management. Our specialized exercises and manual therapy techniques help restore mobility and reduce discomfort in affected joints.

Sport Injury

Expert rehabilitation for athletes and active individuals. We provide targeted treatment for muscle strains, ligament injuries, and sports-related conditions to get you back in the game.

Cervical Spondylitis

Specialized care for neck pain and cervical spine disorders. Our treatment includes posture correction, strengthening exercises, and pain relief techniques to improve neck mobility.

Lumbar Spondylitis

Effective management of lower back pain and inflammation. We use combination therapies including exercises, manual therapy, and modalities to reduce pain and improve function.

Slip Disc

Non-surgical treatment for herniated and bulging discs. Our approach includes traction, core strengthening, and posture correction to relieve nerve compression and restore normal function.

Tennis Elbow

Targeted therapy for lateral epicondylitis. We provide ultrasound therapy, stretching exercises, and strengthening programs to relieve elbow pain and restore grip strength.

Paralysis

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs for stroke and neurological conditions. Our multidisciplinary approach focuses on motor re-education, functional training, and improving independence.

Muscular Problems

Treatment for muscle pain, spasms, and weakness. We address conditions like fibromyalgia, muscle strains, and myofascial pain through soft tissue mobilization and therapeutic exercises.

Frozen Shoulder

Progressive treatment for adhesive capsulitis. Our protocol includes gentle mobilization, stretching exercises, and modalities to restore shoulder range of motion and reduce stiffness.

Sciatica

Effective management of sciatic nerve pain. We use nerve gliding exercises, McKenzie techniques, and core stabilization to reduce leg pain and restore normal walking patterns.

Women's Health Care

Specialized physiotherapy for women's health issues including pelvic floor dysfunction, incontinence, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Gentle and effective treatment tailored to women's needs.

Pregnancy Care

Comprehensive support before, during, and after pregnancy. We provide safe exercises, posture correction, pelvic floor training, and postpartum rehabilitation to ensure optimal health and recovery.
